ABOUT ME

I’m an independent music producer in Chicago with over ten years of experience. I love helping artists achieve the sound they hear in their head, whether it’s with a light touch or with heavier production work. I play bass, guitars, keys, and percussion. In addition, I’m a recording, mixing, and mastering engineer. I’m comfortable with any genre, especially pop, electronic, and alternative.
